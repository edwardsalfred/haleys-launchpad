A few minutes about Haley, summer learning, and why we built this together.

This summer, I wanted to give my granddaughter Haley something better than a stack of workbooks. She's heading into 3rd grade, and I wanted her to walk in on day one feeling ready, not because she crammed, but because she'd actually had fun getting there.

So I built her a course. Real Mississippi 3rd grade standards for Math and Reading, turned into missions on a little rocket ship, with badges and stars along the way. She picks it up on her mom's phone whenever she wants, with no login trouble and no waiting on me to sit down with her every time.

It worked so well for Haley that I wanted other families to have it too. Every mission is still standards-aligned underneath the fun. That part never changed. I hope it helps your cadet the way it's helped mine.

With love,Haley's Grandpa

How Haley's Launchpad works

Real 3rd grade standards, wrapped in a space adventure a kid actually wants to open.

🪐

40 missions, 2 galaxies

Every Grade 3 math standard plus a full reading unit on character & story, split into ~20-minute, Pomodoro-sized missions.

🔓

Mastery gating

Kids score 80%+ on a mission's quiz to unlock the next one, so there's no skipping ahead before a skill sticks, and unlimited retakes with zero shame.

⭐

Real gamification

XP, ranks, daily streaks, and collectible badges layered right on top of the standards. Not a separate "game mode." The learning itself is the game.

🔊

Every screen read aloud

Built for a kid working solo, with narration on every lesson and quiz question so reading level is never the bottleneck.

👨‍👩‍👧

A dashboard for you

See progress, streaks, and stars at a glance, and read your cadet's writing, all behind a simple grown-ups gate so it stays their space.

🔒

Kid-safe login

One parent email account, then each cadet gets a picture avatar and a 4-digit code. No email address needed for your child.
